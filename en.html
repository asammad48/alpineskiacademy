FAQS: We Resolve Your Key Questions for Skiing in Baqueira

I'm not sure of my ski/snowboard level, how do I choose the right lesson?

No worries! Our expert team will help you. Contact us , tell us about your previous experience, or send us a video of your last ski trip to our WhatsApp (+34 669 911 342) . With that information, we will recommend the ideal lesson in Baqueira so you can make the most of every minute, ensuring the choice is the most appropriate for your progression.

How early should I book my lessons?

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance, especially during peak season (December-February). Last-minute bookings are sometimes available, but popular time slots fill up quickly.

What is your cancellation policy for ski lessons?

We understand that unforeseen circumstances may arise. You can cancel your lessons free of charge if you notify us with at least 24 hours' notice. Likewise, if lessons cannot be given due to total closure of the resort or for meteorological safety reasons, we will proceed with a refund or seek an alternative. Check our full cancellation policy here. This policy applies exclusively to our lessons; associated services are governed by the policies of each provider.

Is it mandatory to wear a helmet for skiing in Baqueira or in your lessons?

Although not legally mandatory for adults on slopes, at Alpine Ski Academy we strongly recommend it for everyone. For children, it is mandatory, in addition to recommending back protectors. In freestyle or freeride lessons, helmets are also mandatory. on the snow is our top priority. Ski smart, ski safe!

Do you offer special discounts or promotions on ski lessons in Baqueira?

At Alpine Ski Academy, we are committed to a transparent pricing policy and exceptional value. Our rates are the result of a careful annual market study, positioning us competitively. We offer premium quality at a fair price, without increases due to high demand. If you book 3-4 hour lessons or a full day, the price per hour improves slightly. Additionally, afternoon lessons have a reduction of €5/hour. Remember: the afternoon ski pass is also cheaper, so afternoon lessons have a better combined rate.
