Why Organize Your Ski Lesson with Us

Frequently Asked Questions about Lessons for Companies and Groups

How many people are needed to form a private group?

There is no mandatory minimum. We have organized classes from small teams of 4 people to groups of more than 50 skiers.

The important thing is to clearly define the objectives, levels, and logistics. We adapt to what you need, regardless of the size of your group.

Can we combine skiing and snowboarding in the same group?

Yes, as long as the number of students per discipline allows it and we can assign specialized instructors.

We organize both ski and snowboard lessons, with the same professionalism and adaptation for each discipline.
