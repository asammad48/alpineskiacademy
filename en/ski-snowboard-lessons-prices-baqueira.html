How much does a ski lesson cost in Baqueira?

When you're looking for the price of a ski lesson in Baqueira, it's easy to focus only on the duration or the number of students. But there's a lot more at play: the level, the type of lesson, the date, the days, and even the technology we apply.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we're committed to a personalised, professional, and high-quality teaching model, and this is reflected in each of our prices. Our lessons are designed for those who value real follow-up, refined technique, and a carefully curated experience from start to finish.

Here we offer a clear overview of our prices and everything they include. If you have any doubts or special needs, our team will be delighted to help you choose the best option for you, your children, or your group.

We want to offer you the best experience-to-price ratio in the sector!

How We Set Our Prices

Each season, our prices are updated after a thorough analysis of the market and the more than 20 schools operating in Baqueira.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we understand that the price of a ski lesson must be in line with the real value the student receives: professional, personalised teaching, with attention to detail and a firm commitment to your learning.

That's why we set our prices always seeking a coherent relationship between what we offer and what you get.

Our objective is to provide you with a high-level technical and human experience, at a fair price, guaranteeing the best quality-price ratio in the sector.

Frequently Asked Questions about Prices

Do lessons include equipment or lift pass?

No. Our prices cover exclusively the ski or snowboard instruction service. Equipment rental and lift passes are not included.

However, we advise you free of charge with recommended shops and options so you can easily get what you need.

Do prices change according to the season?

No. At Alpine Ski Academy, we keep our prices stable throughout the season, without extra costs on key dates such as Christmas or Easter.

This way, you can plan your ski lessons in Baqueira without surprises or last-minute variations.
