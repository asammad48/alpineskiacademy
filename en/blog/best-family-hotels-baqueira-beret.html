The Best Family Hotels and Apartments in the Aran Valley: Our Recommendations for the 25-26 Season

  1. Home
  2. Blog
  3. Best Family Hotels in Baqueira Beret

Editor's Note: At Alpine Ski Academy, we review and update our recommendations year after year to ensure that our guide is always the most accurate and relevant for your trip. This is our selection for the 25-26 season, with all the news and corrections.

Introduction: Your Expert Ski and Accommodation Guide

At Alpine Ski Academy, we know that your ski experience doesn't end on the slopes. The place where you rest and recharge is key to fully enjoying your vacation in Baqueira Beret. Over the years, we have met the best hospitality professionals in the Aran Valley and have carefully selected the hotels and apartments that best suit families and groups of skiers.

Therefore, as your guides and instructors on the snow, we share a selection of accommodations with a family atmosphere, guaranteed quality, and the proximity you need to make the most of every minute on the mountain. These are hotels and apartments of local people, who live the Aran Valley and who will make you feel at home, offering you an authentic and personal experience. If you still don't have your class, discover our offer of ski and snowboard lessons and book your experience.

1. Hotels and Apartments for Lovers of Tradition and the Mountains

These accommodations combine the rustic charm of the Valley with all the comforts.

Hotel Seixes de Bagergue

Bagergue

Tranquility and Dream Views

Located in the highest village in the Aran Valley, Hotel Seixes offers rooms with stunning views of the mountains and the Aneto. Its cozy atmosphere makes it a perfect option for those looking to combine skiing, mountains, and tranquility.

Instructor's tip: If you stay here, mention that you come from Alpine Ski Academy to receive a very special treat.

Discover Hotel Seixes de Bagergue

Hotel Deth País in Salardú

Salardú

Proximity to Slopes and Local Cuisine

This family hotel offers comfortable rooms and high-quality services, located near the ski slopes of Baqueira Beret. Its rooms are spacious and the hotel has a restaurant with local cuisine.

Learn about Hotel Deth País

Garos Ostau

Aran Valley

Hospitality and Family Atmosphere

This charming accommodation offers cozy rooms and personalized services. It is known for its hospitality and family atmosphere, with rustic-style rooms and views of the Aran Valley.

Instructor's tip: When contacting Alicia, mention that you are recommended by Alpine Ski Academy and you will receive even better attention.

Visit Garos Ostau

2. Apartments for Families and Groups

If you are traveling with your family or a group of friends, an apartment offers you the space and freedom you need.

Vista Bagergue Apartment

Bagergue

The Perfect Place for Large Families

Enjoy one of the best views of the Aran Valley in this cozy apartment. Perfect for large families or groups, it has 3 bedrooms, 2 bathrooms, and a spacious living room with a fireplace to relax after a day of skiing.

Instructor's tip: Don't forget to mention that you come from Alpine Ski Academy to get a 5% discount on your accommodation!

See Vista Bagergue Apartment

Es De Pau Casa Rural

Aran Valley

Authentic High Mountain Experience

This rural house has a capacity for 6 people, a fully equipped kitchen, a fireplace, and a patio with a barbecue to enjoy the outdoors and nature.

Book your stay at Es De Pau Casa Rural

3. Hotels with Connection to the Snow: Ski Right from the Door!

These accommodations are designed for skiers and offer maximum comfort to get to the slopes.

Baqueira Beret

Direct Access to the Slopes

Located in a beautiful natural environment, this hotel offers spacious apartments and direct access to the slopes of Baqueira Beret. It has play areas for children and a shuttle service to the station.

Why Choose Our Recommended Accommodations?

  • Proximity to the Slopes: All are strategically located so you can make the most of your time on the snow.
  • Family Environments: Most have specific services for families, ensuring a comfortable and fun stay for everyone.
  • Quality and Comfort: They have been selected for their quality and the amenities they offer.
  • Support for Local Businesses: By choosing these accommodations, you support local professionals who will make you feel like part of their family. These businesses are the heart of the Aran Valley.

Frequently Asked Questions about Hotels in Baqueira Beret

What family hotels do you recommend near the slopes of Baqueira Beret?

At Alpine Ski Academy, we recommend hotels managed by local hoteliers such as Hotel Seixes in Bagergue, Garos Ostau, and Hotel Deth País in Salardú, all of which are very close to the slopes and have an authentic family atmosphere. For those who prefer more independence, we highlight the Vista Bagergue Apartment and Es De Pau Casa Rural, ideal for families and groups who want to cook and have more space during their stay.

Is it better to stay in a hotel or an apartment in the Aran Valley?

It depends on your travel plan. Hotels offer comfort, extra services, and personalized attention that makes you feel at home. Apartments are a great option for families or groups looking for flexibility, additional space, and the possibility of preparing their own meals.
