Introduction: Your Ski and Gastronomy Guide in the Valley

At our ski school, our passion for the mountains goes beyond the slopes. We know that a great day of skiing in Baqueira Beret is not complete without a memorable meal. The Aran Valley is a gastronomic paradise, and over the years, we have discovered culinary gems in each of its villages.

Therefore, as your guides and instructors on the snow, we share our personal and sincere selection of the best restaurants, divided by areas. Here you will find options for all tastes, from traditional high mountain cuisine to innovative proposals that will leave you speechless. And of course, always with the appetite that a day of skiing opens up.
