Cancellation Policy

Last updated:

General Cancellation Conditions

At Alpine Ski Academy, we accept cancellations at least 24 hours in advance, provided there is a justified reason, such as:

  • Injuries or medical issues
  • Trip cancellations due to major causes
  • Other force majeure circumstances properly documented

Weather-Related Cancellations

Cancellations due to weather conditions will only be accepted if:

  • The ski resort is officially closed, or
  • Weather conditions are deemed dangerous for skiing by our technical team

No refunds will be issued for bad weather conditions if the resort remains open and lessons can proceed safely.

Scope of Responsibility

Alpine Ski Academy is only responsible for ski lesson services booked directly with us.

Any additional services —such as equipment rental, restaurant bookings, transport, accommodation, etc.— are managed by third-party providers and are subject to their own cancellation and booking policies.

Cancellation Procedure

To request a cancellation, you must notify us in writing (preferably via WhatsApp or email), stating the reason and attaching any relevant documentation if needed.

Cancellations made less than 24 hours in advance are not eligible for a refund.

Timeline Summary

More than 24 hours: Cancellation accepted with valid justification
Less than 24 hours: Not eligible for refund
