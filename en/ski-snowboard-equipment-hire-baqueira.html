Where to hire skis and snowboards in Baqueira?

If you're coming to ski in Baqueira and don't have your own gear, hiring the right equipment is key to fully enjoying the experience.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we are a ski and snowboard school, not a rental shop, but we offer a complementary and free service: personal advice to help you choose the most suitable equipment for your level, your discipline, and the resort's conditions.

We collaborate with establishments located in strategic and very convenient areas, both in Ruda and Vielha, which have new and updated equipment, adapted for all levels: from beginners to expert piste, all-mountain, freeride, or freestyle skiers.

Free Advisory Service!

When you confirm your ski or snowboard lesson booking, if you're interested in this service, we'll send you a WhatsApp link with: how to get to the recommended shops, opening hours, contact details, recommendations based on your level, and an exclusive perk for coming from Alpine Ski Academy.

Our free advisory service

At Alpine Ski Academy, we help you choose the ideal equipment for you at no extra cost. This service is included with any booking of our ski or snowboard lessons.

For Beginners

We'll tell you what type of skis, boots, and protection to ask for. If you need ski lessons for children or beginner adults, our advice will be a great help.

For Families

We'll guide you on helmets, sizing, and safety to ensure group ski lessons for children or private lessons are a success.

For Experts

We'll help you choose between piste skis, all-mountain, freeride or freestyle based on your profile. If you're interested in Carv technology, the equipment is also key.

Just tell us your height, level, and type of lesson, and we'll take care of telling you what kind of equipment suits you.

Because a good lesson starts with good equipment choice.

Types of recommended equipment based on your profile

Below, we detail the type of equipment we usually recommend for each skier profile, ensuring you optimise your learning and enjoyment on the slopes.

Beginner (children and adult beginners)

  • Soft skis, short radius, and narrow waist
  • Easy to turn, not very demanding
  • Comfortable boots, not excessively rigid
  • Helmet compulsory
  • For children: skis shorter than their height and back protectors if necessary

Intermediate level (piste and technical improvement)

  • More reactive, but controllable skis
  • Medium radius, waist between 70 and 80 mm
  • Medium stiffness boots adapted to weight
  • Poles suitable for height

Freeride / All-Mountain

  • Longer skis, with greater floatation
  • Wide waist (90+ mm)
  • Solid and stable structure
  • Firm boots with good power transmission

Technical lessons / High level on piste

  • High-end or specific piste skis
  • Radius adapted to the discipline (slalom, giant...)
  • Stiff, well-fitting boots
  • Equipment check recommended before the lesson

Freestyle

  • Twin tip skis (preferably bindings mounted in the centre)
  • Medium/wide waist and moderate flex
  • Boots with good cushioning
  • Helmet, lumbar protection, and knee pads optional

How is the hire organised?

1

When to collect the equipment?

Whenever possible, we recommend collecting the equipment the day before, especially if your lesson is first thing in the morning. This allows you to avoid rushing, calmly try on boots, and make adjustments if necessary.

2

How much time is needed?

If you have to collect it on the same day, we recommend arriving at least 45 minutes before your lesson. Shops open at 8:30 AM, which gives you enough time to organise everything if your ski or snowboard lesson is at 9:30 AM.

3

Where are the shops?

The shops we collaborate with are located in Ruda, Salardú, and Vielha, in very accessible areas. We'll send you a direct link via WhatsApp when you confirm your booking with addresses, opening hours, and how to get there from your accommodation.

4

What should you bring or know?

We'll help you know what equipment you need based on your level. The shops will have it prepared (as much as possible) and adjusted to your profile. And you'll receive an exclusive perk for coming from Alpine Ski Academy.

Frequently asked questions about equipment hire

Is equipment hire included in the lesson price?

No. Equipment hire is an external service. At Alpine Ski Academy, we help you manage it easily and at no additional cost, but payment for the hire is made directly at the shop. In return, you'll receive an exclusive perk for coming from us.

Can I hire equipment on the same day as the lesson?

Yes, although we recommend collecting it the day before if your lesson starts early. Shops open at 8:30 AM, so if you arrive with enough time (about 45 minutes before the lesson), you can also manage it in the morning without a problem.

Need help with your equipment?

Write to us when you make your booking and we'll send you everything you need: shop address, what to ask for, when to collect it, and how to get your exclusive perk.
