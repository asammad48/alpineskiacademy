A New Dimension of Skiing

After so many seasons shared in Baqueira, we know that for many of you, skiing goes far beyond being just a sport. It's disconnection. It's intensity. It's technique, landscape, emotion, health, family. It's a way of living winter through experiences that nourish body and mind, and are remembered for a lifetime.

That's why Alpine Ski Academy Trips was born: a project designed for those who want to continue exploring the world through skiing, with the support of the instructors they already know, in the places they've always dreamed of.

Each season we'll choose an emblematic destination to offer you a complete adventure: technical, cultural, scenic, and human. Places where the alpine ski culture is breathed in every village, on every lift, on every run.

Destination 2025: St. Moritz (Switzerland)

In April 2025, we're starting our first big adventure outside Baqueira with a destination worthy of the occasion: St. Moritz, in the heart of the Swiss Alps.

Frequently Asked Questions about Ski Trips

Is a high level required to participate?

It's not necessary to be an expert skier, but a consolidated intermediate level (red runs with autonomy) is recommended.

If you have doubts, consult us and we'll guide you.

Can I come without booking lessons?

Lessons are included in the trip and don't involve an additional cost.

If you decide not to take advantage of them, the price remains the same, as it's part of the package. Even so, we encourage you to participate: they are adapted, technical, and very enriching sessions.

Are you up for travelling with us?

This isn't just any trip. It's the opportunity to experience skiing like never before: in a legendary destination, with the comfort of a professional organisation and the accompaniment of the instructors you already trust.
