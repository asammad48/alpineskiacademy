At Alpine Ski Academy, we offer you access to Baqueira Beret's live webcams so you can check the current slope and snow conditions before your ski lessons. Although we don't manage the resort, our lessons take place in its facilities, and these cameras will help you better plan your snow experience.

Plan Your Snow Experience

Snow Conditions

Check the cameras to evaluate real snow conditions before booking your lessons. You'll be able to see if there's powder, packed snow, or spring conditions.

Weather Conditions

Observe visibility, sun intensity, and real-time weather conditions to plan the appropriate equipment for your ski day.

Skier Traffic

The cameras allow you to estimate public traffic in different areas of the resort, helping you choose the best times for your lessons.
