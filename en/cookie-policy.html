Cookie Policy

Alpine Ski Academy

1. What are cookies?

Cookies are small text files that websites store on a user's device (computer, tablet, or mobile) while browsing the Internet. Their main function is to remember information about the visit, such as language preferences, login details, or display settings, to improve the user experience.

2. Types of cookies used

The Alpine Ski Academy website uses its own and third-party cookies for technical, analytical, and advertising purposes. The categories are detailed below:

  • Technical or necessary cookies: Allow navigation and use of the different options or services offered by the website.
  • Analysis or measurement cookies: Allow quantifying the number of users and analyzing their use of the website to improve its content and services.
  • Personalization cookies: Allow remembering user preferences to offer a tailored experience.
  • Advertising or marketing cookies: Allow the management of advertising spaces based on user behavior and browsing habits.
