Processing of Personal Data

In compliance with applicable Data Protection regulations, we inform you that the data to be processed by Víctor Puche Fita (hereinafter "Baqueira Beret Ski Academy") is for the purpose of providing the service requested by the user through our contact form, and for this, you need to check the box indicating acceptance of this policy. For the proper provision of the services offered by Baqueira Beret Ski Academy, the user declares that they are of legal age.

The processing of personal data requested will also have the purpose of sending commercial information regarding products and services currently and in the future offered by Baqueira Beret Ski Academy; this information includes advertising and promotional communications via postal mail, fax, email, or any other means. To carry out this processing, the user must check the box provided for this purpose on the website form.

If the user subscribes to our newsletter or blog, their email address will be added to our database for sending news. To subscribe to this service, it is necessary to check the subscription box; if not, you will not be subscribed to our newsletter.

Given the personal nature of the data provided, Baqueira Beret Ski Academy commits to treating it with strict confidentiality and maintaining the necessary security measures according to the risk analysis performed.

We inform you that your data will not be transferred to third parties nor will there be international data transfers, except when legally required or if necessary solely for the provision of the requested service, in which case consent will be requested.

Rights

The collected and processed data will be retained for a limited period, necessary to fulfill legal obligations imposed on Baqueira Beret Ski Academy by various applicable regulations.

As an interested party, you can exercise the following rights at any time at the address indicated in the Legal Notice:

  • Right of Access: The interested party may request the Controller to provide details on the personal data processed and, if affirmative, which specific personal data is being processed.
  • Right of Rectification: The interested party may request the Controller to correct their personal data if it is inaccurate.
  • Right of Erasure: The interested party may request the Controller to delete their personal data.
  • Right to Object: The interested party may object to the processing of their personal data.
  • Right to Restriction of Processing: The interested party may request the Controller to temporarily halt processing their personal data in specific cases.
  • Right to Data Portability: The interested party may request the Controller to provide their automated data in a structured, accessible, and manageable format.

For more information about your rights, visit www.agpd.es.

Additionally, you have the right to lodge a complaint with the Spanish Data Protection Agency.
