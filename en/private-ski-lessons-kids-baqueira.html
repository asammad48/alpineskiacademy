What Age and Level Are These Lessons For?

At Alpine Ski Academy, we offer private ski lessons for children from 3 years old up to adolescence. Each stage of child development requires a different approach, and that's why our lessons are precisely adapted to each pupil's level, maturity, energy, and pace.

3 Years

Initiation with Play, Safety, and Own Pace

The main objective is for the child to enjoy themselves, feel safe, and start gliding with joy. We're not looking for a formal lesson, but a positive experience that sparks their curiosity for the snow.

  • Flexible duration: 1-2 hours
  • Pace set by the child
  • Adaptation to family routines
  • Possibility of hotel pick-up
4-6 Years

Safety, Play, and Technical Progress

Children show greater autonomy and motor coordination. We continue to use play as a central tool, but with focus on balance, braking and basic wedge turns.

  • Gentle and safe areas
  • Introduction of 'cuñeros' and back protectors
  • Development of balance and control
  • Areas: Carreretes, Cabana, beginners
7-10 Years

Technique, Confidence and Autonomy

Consolidation of technique: evolved wedge turns, introduction to parallel, speed control and autonomy with simple lifts.

  • Combination of technique and play
  • Gentle challenges in new areas
  • Introduction to Fun Park
  • Development of autonomy
10+ Years

Consolidation and New Challenges

We work on more advanced technical objectives according to the pupil's level: complex turns, basic carving, adaptation to different snow types and snowpark.

  • Dynamic and challenging approach
  • Advanced technique
  • Era Marmota Snowpark
  • Reinforcement of autonomy

What Does Your Child Need for Their First Lesson?

At Alpine Ski Academy, we make sure everything is easy for you and your child. To ensure the experience is comfortable, safe, and positive from the very first moment, we recommend coming with the right equipment and well-chosen clothing.

Clothing and Protection

  • ✅ Thermal base layer
  • ✅ Fleece or mid-layer
  • ✅ Waterproof trousers and jacket
  • ✅ Warm and waterproof gloves
  • ✅ Hat or buff for under helmet
  • ✅ Goggles or sunglasses
  • ✅ Sun cream and lip balm

Technical Equipment

  • ✅ Child skis with bindings
  • ✅ Comfortable, well-adjusted boots
  • ✅ Helmet mandatory
  • ✅ Back protector recommended
  • ✅ 'Cuñeros' for first times
  • ✅ Poles (if instructor indicates)

What if something's missing? Don't worry. If you don't have 'cuñeros' or need sun cream, we carry these support materials. The rest of the equipment must be brought by the pupil or you can easily rent it.

Frequently Asked Questions About Ski Lessons for Children

❓ What's the minimum age you recommend for starting private ski lessons?

We recommend starting from 3 years old, provided the child walks confidently and doesn't wear nappies.

At these ages, skiing should be a positive experience that respects their pace. That's why our lessons are completely adapted to their needs with flexible duration, gentle and safe areas, play as the main focus, and instructors specialised in child education.

❓ What happens if my child doesn't want to continue or gets overwhelmed during the lesson?

It's completely normal for some children to feel insecure or simply have an off day.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we never force situations. Our instructors are trained to manage emotions with empathy, patience, and play. If the child needs to stop, rest, or simply observe for a while, we respect that. We always prioritise their emotional well-being.
