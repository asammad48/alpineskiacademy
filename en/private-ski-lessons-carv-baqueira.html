Why Choose a Lesson with Carv Technology

The Carv system is not an accessory. It is a high-level technical analysis tool that, when combined with the experience of a good instructor, allows for tangible improvements in less time. At Alpine Ski Academy, we don't just use it: we master it.

Frequently Asked Questions about using Carv

❓ Do I need to have my own Carv for this class?

No. We provide you with the Carv sensor during the class—we install it directly in your boots, which are compatible without exception.

And if you already have your own Carv, the experience will be even more complete: we will work with your previous data and configure the settings to optimize each analysis.

❓ What is the difference between Carv and a traditional technical class?

Carv provides data that no human eye can record in real time.

During the class, we will measure and analyze with you variables such as: – Parallelism and turn shape – Early edging and amount of angle – Progressiveness in inclination – Symmetry between left and right turns – Fore/aft balance – G-forces generated in each turn

This information, interpreted by our expert Carv instructors, is transformed into immediate feedback and personalized exercises that allow you to improve with unprecedented precision.
