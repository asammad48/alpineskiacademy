Fancy a day on the slopes all together, without splitting up the group or sacrificing a quality lesson? At Alpine Ski Academy ski school, we offer private lessons designed for families who want to learn, improve, and enjoy sharing the experience.

Whether you've got kids who ski better than their parents, or someone's starting from scratch, our instructors know how to adapt the session so everyone progresses at their own pace, with safety, fun, and a real sense of connection. We arrange the lesson so that whoever needs it most gets priority attention, ensuring no one gets left behind.

Because beyond the technique, family skiing creates unique bonds and memories that'll stick with you forever.

Families Enjoying Together in the Snow

Why Choose a Family Ski Lesson in Baqueira with Alpine Ski Academy

Private family lessons don't just allow you to learn together; they create a special connection between parents, children, siblings, or cousins. At Alpine Ski Academy, we design each lesson so that all group members can enjoy themselves, progress, and feel comfortable, regardless of their level or age.

Exclusive Instructor for the Whole Family

You'll have an instructor just for yourselves, who'll be with you throughout the lesson, adapting to the group. This exclusive attention allows for real-time adjustments to the dynamics, making the most of every minute on the slopes.

Coordinated Paces and Levels for Everyone's Progress

A family lesson can include complete beginners, intermediates, or advanced skiers. Our job is to balance the lesson so everyone makes progress: sometimes with individual exercises, sometimes with shared activities.

Quality Time Together on the Snow

There's no need to split the group. With our method, we create shared moments even if levels differ. This way, you enjoy the mountain together, without complications or rushing.

Total Flexibility in Your Ski Experience

We can start the lesson from your accommodation, arrange pick-up or drop-off for the little ones, or adjust the schedule if needed. We coordinate everything in advance so you don't have to worry about a thing.

What Kind of Family Are You? We Adapt the Lesson to Your Group and Needs

At Alpine Ski Academy, there's no single way to ski as a family. That's why we design each lesson based on your group's characteristics: ages, levels, goals, and even the emotional dynamic among its members.

Beginner Parents and Experienced Skiing Kids?

It's very common for children to have an advantage because they learned earlier. In this case, the instructor prioritises the adults in the first few minutes of the lesson with guided exercises, while the children review and warm up.

Then, we bring the whole group back together for sections where everyone can participate. This way, we balance attention and fun for everyone.

Everyone Starting from Scratch in Baqueira?

If everyone's a beginner, brilliant! We create a shared process where everyone progresses at their own pace but with adapted exercises. Shared lessons help build confidence and provide plenty of laughs.

The aim is for you to enjoy yourselves without fear and leave the lesson eager to come back for more.

Very Different Levels in Your Family Group?

If one member has significantly more experience, we offer two options:

  • Do specific exercises while the group reviews technique
  • Divide temporary sections within the lesson, alternating focused attention

In any case, we make sure everyone feels valued and attended to.

Frequently Asked Questions about Family Ski Lessons in Baqueira

❓ Is it possible to have a family lesson even if we have different levels?

Yes, absolutely. It's one of the most common scenarios. Our instructors are prepared to adapt the lesson according to the group's needs, alternating moments where they work with each person or sharing exercises that benefit everyone.

The goal is for the experience to be balanced and enriching for everyone, without frustration or downtime.

❓ From what age can a child participate in a family lesson?

We recommend that children are at least 7 or 8 years old and have a certain minimum level of autonomy to participate in lessons with adults. If the little ones are still complete beginners or require a more playful approach, it's ideal for them to start with a specific kids' lesson.

However, every family is different: we evaluate each case to adapt the lesson to what truly works.
