Frequently Asked Questions about Lessons for Businesses and Groups

How many people are needed to form a private group?

There's no mandatory minimum. We've organised lessons from small teams of 4 people to groups of over 50 skiers.

The important thing is to clearly define objectives, levels and logistics. We adapt to what you need, regardless of your group size.

Can we combine skiing and snowboarding in the same group?

Yes, provided the number of students per discipline allows it and we can assign specialised instructors.

We organise both ski lessons and snowboard lessons, with the same professionalism and adaptation for each discipline.
