Fancy learning to ski or honing your technique without being alone on the slopes? Alpine Ski Academy's group lessons for adults are an excellent alternative to private lessons: technical progression, a relaxed atmosphere, and a more accessible price, all with qualified instructors and dynamics tailored to your level.

We organise homogeneous groups by level, with morning and afternoon slots, and a maximum of 8 students per class. It's a perfect opportunity to meet other skiers, improve your skiing, and get more out of every day in Baqueira.

Adult Groups in Action

Advantages of Our Adult Group Lessons

Our adult group lessons are designed for you to learn or improve safely, with good technique and in a motivating environment. It doesn't matter if it's your first time or if you've skied before: we group you by level so you can make the most of the experience.

Technical Learning in a Group

Each lesson focuses on clear progression: posture, turns, speed control, balance, and adapting to the terrain. Even though you're in a group, the instructor provides individual corrections and precise technical feedback.

Homogeneous Level and Common Pace

We group by level and experience, so you feel comfortable, progress at a good pace, and don't get frustrated. You can send us a small video if you have any doubts about your ideal group.

Alternative to Private Lessons

A more economical option, but with the same technical demands. Ideal for those who want to improve without needing exclusive attention or simply enjoy learning more in a group. For other options, consult our private adult ski lessons.

Morning and Afternoon Groups

Choose the slot that best suits your day:

  • Morning: 9:30 AM–12:30 PM
  • Afternoon: 1:00 PM–4:00 PM

The afternoon is ideal if you want to ski with fewer crowds and take advantage of a reduced lift pass.

How Our Adult Group Ski Lessons Work

We want you to feel comfortable from the very first minute. Here's how our adult lessons are organised: from the meeting point to group formation and available times.

Meeting Point

Lessons start and finish at a point defined according to the group's level and resort conditions (1800, 1500 or Era Cabana).

Group Formation

Groups by level (beginner, intermediate or advanced) with maximum 8 students. Minimum 4 people to open a group.

Prior Assessment

If you have doubts about your level, you can send us a brief video via WhatsApp to assign you to the most suitable group.

Frequently Asked Questions about Adult Group Ski Lessons

Can I sign up alone or do I need to come with a formed group?

You can sign up alone without a problem. In fact, most adult students enrol individually. We'll take care of assigning you to the most suitable group by level, schedule, and learning pace.

What happens if a group doesn't form in my chosen slot?

For an adult group to form, we need a minimum of 4 participants. Sometimes, especially in low season or for very specific levels, it can be tricky to reach this number. If this happens in your chosen time slot or level, don't worry, we'll always find the best solution for you to enjoy the snow and keep learning.

We'll suggest real and flexible alternatives:

  • Another compatible group: We'll look for a group with a similar level that fits your profile at a nearby time.
  • Reduced group with adapted fee: If there are 2 or 3 participants interested in the same slot and level, we can offer the option of forming a reduced group, adjusting the fee for you.
  • Collaboration with partner schools: If none of the above options fit, we'll help you find a trusted partner school in Baqueira that we collaborate with, where there might be a group that suits your needs.

Our goal is for you to always be able to ski and learn, without complications. Check our prices and rates for ski and snowboard lessons in Baqueira for more information.
