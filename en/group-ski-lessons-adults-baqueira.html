Do you want to learn to ski or perfect your technique without being alone on the slopes? The group lessons for adults at Alpine Ski Academy are an excellent alternative to private lessons: technical progress, a relaxed atmosphere, and a more affordable price, all with certified instructors and dynamics adapted to your level.

We organize homogeneous groups by level, in morning and afternoon schedules, with a maximum of 8 students per class. It is a perfect opportunity to meet other skiers, improve your skiing, and enjoy each day in Baqueira more.

Adult Groups in Action

Advantages of Our Group Lessons for Adults

The group lessons for adults are designed for you to learn or improve with safety, good technique, and in a motivating environment. It doesn't matter if it's your first time or if you have skied before: we group you by level so you can make the most of the experience.

Technical Learning in a Group

In each class, a clear progression is worked on: posture, turns, speed control, supports, and adaptation to the terrain. Although you are in a group, the instructor corrects individually and gives precise technical feedback.

Homogeneous Level and Common Pace

We group by level and experience, so you feel comfortable, progress at a good pace, and don't get frustrated. You can send us a short video if you have doubts about your ideal group.

Alternative to Private Lessons

A more economical option, but with the same technical demand. Ideal for those who want to improve without the need for exclusive attention or simply enjoy learning in a group more. For other options, check our private ski lessons for adults.

Morning and Afternoon Groups

Choose the time that best suits your day:

  • Morning: 9:30 AM – 12:30 PM
  • Afternoon: 1:00 PM – 4:00 PM

The afternoon is ideal if you want to ski with fewer crowds and take advantage of the reduced ski pass.

How Group Ski Lessons for Adults Work

We want you to feel comfortable from the first minute. Here we explain how our adult classes are organized: from the meeting point to the formation of groups and available schedules.

Meeting Point

The classes start and end at a point defined according to the group's level and the station's conditions (1800, 1500, or Era Cabana).

Group Formation

Groups by level (beginner, intermediate, or advanced) with a maximum of 8 students. Minimum of 4 people to open a group.

Preliminary Assessment

If you have doubts about your level, you can send us a short video via WhatsApp to assign you to the most suitable group.

Frequently Asked Questions about Group Ski Lessons for Adults

Can I sign up alone or do I have to come with a formed group?

You can come alone without any problem. In fact, most adult students sign up individually. We take care of assigning you to the most appropriate group by level, schedule, and learning pace.

What happens if a group is not formed in the slot I choose?

For an adult group to be formed, we need a minimum of 4 participants. Sometimes, especially in the low season or for very specific levels, it can be difficult to reach this number. If this happens in the time slot or level you have chosen, don't worry, we will always look for the best solution so you can enjoy the snow and continue learning.

We will propose real and flexible alternatives:

  • Another compatible group: We will look for a group with a similar level that fits your profile at a nearby time.
  • Small group with adapted rate: If there are 2 or 3 participants interested in the same slot and level, we can offer the option of forming a small group, adjusting the rate for you.
  • Collaboration with partner schools: If none of the above options fit, we will help you find a trusted school in Baqueira with which we collaborate, where there is a group that suits your needs.

Our goal is for you to always be able to ski and learn, without complications. Check our ski and snowboard lesson rates and prices in Baqueira for more information.
