Who We Are

At Alpine Ski Academy, we are ski and snowboard instructors in Baqueira with a clear mission: to help you grow on the snow, whatever your level.

Our team is made up of qualified professionals with years of experience in the technical teaching of alpine skiing and snowboarding. But beyond the qualifications, what defines us is our passion for teaching, a close relationship, and a methodology adapted to each person.

We work in one of the most privileged environments in the Pyrenees: the Baqueira Beret resort, where we provide personalized, dynamic, and safe lessons, with a progressive and results-oriented approach.

If you are looking for a ski instructor in Baqueira who combines technical knowledge, real experience, and a genuine connection with the mountains, you are in the right place.

Our Story on the Snow

As professionals in skiing and snowboarding, we have been teaching in Baqueira since 2001. During this time, we have witnessed the evolution of the resort from the inside and have trained in the best schools in the valley, where we grew as skiers and as people.

Many of us started by taking lessons from great legends of the snow. Years later, that vocation led us to become trainers ourselves, first in other academies and finally creating our own project: Alpine Ski Academy.

This school is born from experience, a commitment to technical teaching, and the desire to offer truly personalized guidance.

Baqueira is not just our workplace: it is our home, our starting point, and the environment that has shaped our way of understanding the mountains.

Philosophy, Vision, and Methodology

At Alpine Ski Academy, we believe that each person has a unique way of learning, which is why we design lessons that adapt to the pace, level, and goals of each student.

Our philosophy is based on respect for individual processes, safe progression, and technique as a tool to enjoy the mountains more. We apply a progressive teaching method, based on active observation, constant feedback, and the use of technical resources adapted to each learning phase. We work with children who are just starting, adults seeking confidence, and technical skiers who want to perfect their level or prepare for official tests.

We don't just "give lessons": we train conscious, autonomous, and motivated skiers. We prioritize experiential learning, understanding movement, and real-time decision-making.

Our approach is personal, technical, and human. We get involved in each learning process as if it were our own. Teaching is not just our profession: it is our commitment to those who, like us, have made the snow a part of their lives.

Our Team of Instructors

Our team consists of certified alpine ski and snowboard instructors with extensive teaching experience and a solid technical foundation. We all hold official certifications recognized by the Spanish and international sports education system (TD1, TD2, TD3), and we also act as trainers for sports technicians at centers like SAFE Formación, where we teach the TD1 and TD2 cycles, as well as the Ski Mastery subject at INEFC (4th year).

Frequently Asked Questions

What should you consider when choosing the best ski instructor in Baqueira?

Choosing the right ski instructor in Baqueira can make the difference between a more or less fun experience… and one that truly helps you progress, gain confidence, and enjoy the mountains more.

Although the official qualification (TD1, TD2, or TD3) is fundamental – as it guarantees technical, safety, and pedagogical knowledge – it is not the only factor that matters. A good instructor must also have on-field experience, know how to read the resort's conditions, adapt to the weather, the equipment, and, above all, the student. Empathy, clear communication, a real commitment to learning, and the ability to inspire motivation are essential qualities.

In short, the best instructor is not just the one who knows the most, but the one who teaches best, accompanies, and adapts to each person. This is precisely what we look for in our team at Alpine Ski Academy: qualification, yes, but also humanity, vocation, and dedication.

How to choose the best ski school in Baqueira?

Choosing the best ski school in Baqueira does not just depend on the name or size of the establishment. There are several key factors that will help you make an informed decision:

  • That it has officially certified instructors
  • That it offers personalized and real student follow-up
  • That it works with technology applied to learning (like Carv or video analysis)
  • That it has a consolidated experience in the resort
  • That it has verified reviews and real testimonials

Often, opting for a small, specialized school is a great advantage: the attention is direct, communication is fluid, the lessons are more carefully planned, and the team really knows each student.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we believe in this model: a technical and rigorous teaching, but also a close one, with real people accompanying other people in their learning on the snow.
