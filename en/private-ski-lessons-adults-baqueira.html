At Alpine Ski Academy, we know every adult has a unique story with skiing. Some are starting from scratch, others aim to perfect their technique or regain confidence after a fall, and many simply want to enjoy the mountains more, with complete safety and control.

Whatever your starting point, our private adult ski lessons are tailor-made for you. With a certified instructor by your side, we adapt each session to your level, age, physical condition, and goals.

Our Adult Students in Action

Why Choose a Private Lesson as an Adult in Baqueira?

Teaching an adult to ski is different from teaching a child. It requires sensitivity, active listening, technical precision, and a methodology that adapts to each pace and expectation.

100% Personalised Attention

Your instructor is dedicated exclusively to you. We assess your initial level, identify potential blocks and define together what you want to achieve.

Visible Technical Progress

We help you understand and improve your basic technique, posture, turn execution and downhill control. You'll notice improvement from the first lesson.

Confidence and Safety

If you have fear after a fall or time without skiing, we work on your confidence with patience, progressive exercises and well-chosen slopes.

Lessons at Your Pace

You choose duration, schedule and meeting point. Total flexibility for your comfort in Baqueira, Beret or Bonaigua.

What's Your Level? We Adapt the Lesson to You

Every adult experiences skiing differently. At Alpine Ski Academy, we adjust each lesson not only to your technical level, but also to your confidence, physical condition and personal goals.

Beginner

Discover Skiing with Safety and Good Sensations

If it's your first time on snow or you haven't skied in years, we start with the essentials: balance, gliding, stopping and wedge turns. All on easy slopes with constant accompaniment so you feel safe from the beginning.

  • Use of lifts (magic carpet to beginner chairlift)
  • Comfortable and controlled wedge turns
  • Managing first descents without fear
  • Constant and personalised accompaniment
Intermediate

Improve Your Technique and Gain Autonomy

If you've already skied and can handle blue slopes, but feel you repeat mistakes or lack fluidity, this lesson will help you consolidate your technical foundations and gain confidence in different conditions.

  • Improving balance and outside edge pressure
  • Refining turns: advanced wedge and transition to parallel
  • Working on different terrains and snow types
  • Terrain reading and rhythm management
Advanced

Master Difficult Snow and Your Own Technique

For experienced skiers looking to take their skiing to the next level. We perfect advanced technique and adaptation to challenging conditions.

  • Short turns at high rhythm and carving curves
  • Managing fog, ice and spring snow
  • Skiing in moguls or controlled off-piste
  • Video-analysis and Carv technology available

Frequently Asked Questions about Adult Ski Lessons

‚ùì Can I start skiing even if I've never done it before and I'm already an adult?

Yes, of course. It's never too late to start! In fact, many of our adult students discover skiing at 40, 50 or even 60 years old.

Our instructors are prepared to teach you from scratch, with a respectful, pressure-free approach totally adapted to your pace. You'll learn to feel comfortable on skis, to stop, turn and enjoy safely from day one.

‚ùì I've had a fall or negative experience... can you help me regain confidence?

Yes. In fact, it's one of our specialities. Many adults come with fear, stiffness or doubts after an injury or unpleasant experience.

At Alpine Ski Academy we design private lessons to gradually regain positive sensations, using gentle slopes, specific exercises and close accompaniment. We don't force progress: we guide it with confidence and technique, ensuring your well-being.
