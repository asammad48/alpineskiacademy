At Alpine Ski Academy, we know that every adult has a unique story with skiing. Some are starting from scratch, others want to perfect their technique or regain confidence after a fall, and many simply want to enjoy the mountain more, with complete safety and control.

Whatever your starting point, our private ski lessons for adults are tailor-made for you. With a certified instructor by your side, we adapt each session to your level, age, fitness, and goals.

Our Adult Students in Action

Why Choose a Private Lesson as an Adult in Baqueira?

Teaching an adult to ski is different from teaching a child. It requires sensitivity, active listening, technical precision, and a methodology that adapts to each pace and expectation.

100% Personalized Attention

Your instructor is dedicated exclusively to you. We assess your initial level, identify potential blocks, and together we define what you want to achieve.

Visible Technical Progress

We help you understand and improve your basic technique, posture, turn execution, and control on descents. You will notice improvement from the first class.

Confidence and Safety

If you are afraid after a fall or haven't skied in a while, we work on your confidence with patience, progressive exercises, and well-chosen slopes.

Lessons at Your Pace

You choose the duration, schedule, and meeting point. Total flexibility for your convenience in Baqueira, Beret, or Bonaigua.

What's Your Level? We Adapt the Class to You

Every adult experiences skiing differently. At Alpine Ski Academy, we adjust each class not only to your technical level but also to your confidence, fitness, and personal goals.

Beginner

Discover Skiing with Safety and Good Sensations

If it's your first time on the snow or it's been years since you've skied, we start with the essentials: balance, gliding, braking, and wedge turns. All on easy slopes with constant support to make you feel safe from the start.

  • Using lifts (from magic carpet to beginner chairlift)
  • Comfortable and controlled wedge turns
  • Managing first descents without fear
  • Constant and personalized support
Intermediate

Improve Your Technique and Gain Autonomy

If you've skied before and can handle blue slopes, but feel you're repeating mistakes or lack fluidity, this class will help you consolidate your technical foundations and gain confidence in different conditions.

  • Improving balance and outside ski support
  • Refining turns: advanced wedge and transition to parallel
  • Working on different terrains and snow types
  • Reading the terrain and managing pace
Advanced

Master Difficult Snow and Your Own Technique

For experienced skiers looking to take their skiing to the next level. We perfect advanced technique and adaptation to challenging conditions.

  • Short turns at high pace and carving turns
  • Managing fog, ice, and spring snow
  • Controlled skiing in moguls or off-piste
  • Video analysis and Carv technology available

Frequently Asked Questions about Adult Ski Lessons

‚ùì Can I start skiing even if I've never done it before and I'm an adult?

Yes, of course. It's never too late to start! In fact, many of our adult students discover skiing in their 40s, 50s, or even 60s.

Our instructors are prepared to teach you from scratch, with a respectful, no-pressure approach totally adapted to your pace. You will learn to feel comfortable on skis, to brake, turn, and enjoy with safety from day one.

‚ùì I've had a fall or a negative experience... can you help me regain my confidence?

Yes. In fact, it's one of our specialties. Many adults come with fear, stiffness, or doubts after an injury or unpleasant experience.

At Alpine Ski Academy, we design private lessons to gradually recover positive sensations, using gentle slopes, specific exercises, and close support. We don't force progress: we guide it with confidence and technique, ensuring your well-being.
