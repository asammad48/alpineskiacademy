Our Little Skiers in Action

Pedagogical Methodology in Our Children's Group Ski Lessons

Group lessons for children require a specific methodology. At Alpine Ski Academy, we don't just teach skiing: we accompany each stage of child development with games, adapted technique, and a lot of affection.

Small Groups by Age and Level

We form small groups (between 3 and 7 children) with peers of similar age and level. This allows for a common pace, better communication, and a more effective class.

Games and Integrated Learning

Through playful dynamics, children work on balance, control, orientation, and autonomy without realizing it. Each class is a new adventure.

Safety and Constant Supervision

Our instructors are not only technical experts: they are trained to care for, guide, and constantly observe. Emotional and physical safety is our priority.

How Group Lessons for Children Work in Baqueira

Exclusive Meeting Point

All children's classes start and end next to the Bosque chairlift (1800) in Baqueira. It is a protected and accessible area where parents can accompany them on arrival and watch them start the class peacefully.

Duration and Adapted Pace

The classes have a standard duration of 3 hours, both in the morning and in the afternoon. During that time, we alternate technical sections with playful dynamics, short breaks, and adaptation to the physical and emotional state of the group.

Frequently Asked Questions

From what age can they join a standard ski group?

Standard group lessons are for children from 4 years old, provided they can already make wedge turns or have previous experience.

If your child is 3 to 5 years old and does not yet turn, we offer a more personalized alternative with two instructors for a very small group. Check availability via WhatsApp for this children's ski initiation option.

How do we ensure that the children's ski group is well-formed?

Before the class, we assess each child's level and, if possible, we use a short video sent via WhatsApp. This way we ensure that the group is homogeneous in age and level, which favors the pace, safety, and enjoyment. For any questions, do not hesitate to contact us.
