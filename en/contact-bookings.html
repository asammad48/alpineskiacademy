What Information to Include for a Quick Response?

To provide you with the most useful and accurate response possible, we recommend including the following details in your inquiry:

Desired date(s)

Help us verify availability for the dates you have in mind.

Number of people

Are you coming alone, as a couple, with friends or with family? This helps us organize the perfect lesson.

Type of lesson

Are you looking for private ski lessons like freestyle, freeride or carve, snowboard lessons, or group lessons?

Ski level or previous experience

This helps us place you in the appropriate group or private lesson to maximize your learning.

Preferred time

Do you prefer morning or afternoon lessons? Your preference helps us organize schedules.

Opening Hours and Season

No matter when you contact us, we're always here to help!

During ski season (winter)

We're available every day from 8:30 to 23:30.

Lesson hours on slopes

During skiing hours (9:00-17:00) we might take a little longer to respond if we're teaching (we're instructors and ski every day) but rest assured we'll reply to your email or WhatsApp within one or two hours.

Off-season

We also respond via WhatsApp and email, though less frequently. Get your next ski holiday planned in advance!

Quick Frequently Asked Questions (FAQ)

We've compiled some of the most common questions to help you quickly:

How far in advance should I book?

The sooner you book, the better! Especially on holidays, long weekends, and peak seasons. Some time slots and lesson types fill up weeks in advance.

We recommend booking at least 2-3 weeks in advance to guarantee availability, especially if you have specific dates in mind.

Can I book multiple lessons at once?

Yes, of course. We'll advise you on organizing a personalized schedule by days and levels, ensuring continuous progress.

In fact, we recommend booking several consecutive lessons to maximize your learning and make the most of your stay in Baqueira.

Where Do We Respond From?

📍 At Alpine Ski Academy we are always skiing around Baqueira, with direct presence on the slopes. This allows us to offer you real and up-to-date knowledge of snow conditions, slopes, and daily conditions.

Location on slopes

You'll find us at the main meeting points in Baqueira Beret: Ruda, 1500, Tanau and 1800.

Real-time knowledge

By being on the slopes daily, we can offer you updated information about snow conditions, best routes and recommended slopes according to your level.

Flexible meeting points

We adapt to meet you at the point that best suits you within the Baqueira Beret ski domain.
