Frequently Asked Questions about Ski Lessons in Baqueira

Which type of class is best to start with if I'm a beginner?

If you're taking your first steps on the snow — whether you're a child or an adult — we recommend a private lesson. You'll receive 100% personalised attention from the very first minute, learn safely, and progress at your own pace. Once you gain confidence, you can combine it with group lessons if you wish.

What's the best age to start skiing?

It's never too early... or too late. At Alpine Ski Academy, we teach children from 3 years old with specialised children's methodology, and we also initiate adults with no prior experience. We adapt each class to the student's age, physical condition, and pace, ensuring every debut is positive, technical, and fun.

First time in Baqueira? Not sure which class to choose?

If you're unsure where to start, send us a message via WhatsApp or contact us through the form. We'd be delighted to help you choose the best option based on your level, goals, and previous experience.
